  • LES ENFANTS TERRIBLES
    S/S 2014/15

    It’s during a trip to Bali-Indonesia that the collection took place.
    I was amazed by the traditional Indonesian fabrics such as the Batik, Ikat, or these shiny golden flowery blue, pink, yellow fabrics used to decorate Temples. That was it, I wanted a collection based on patterns, flowers and colours. There I also
    printed on 15 meters fabrics my last painting to have my own pattern too. I created non-structured clothing, mostly one-size/ no-size with some amplitude, based on the kimono technique. A simple shape, cut that leave freedom to the patterns to express their moves, their history, their sensitivity. This collection is
    definitely an ode to flowers and colours.
