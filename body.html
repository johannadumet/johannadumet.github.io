  • BODY MAPPING

    Barcelona 2012
    Acrylic and ink on random wood

    The body is a limited space which organizes and structures a living being. The area of our insides is a corporal cartography. The regions and territories are created by the accumulation of organics lines, colours, textures and patterns. Our body is a living landscape, a vessel circulating traffic, a growing forest limited by physical borders.


    Exhibition at Mutuo Centro Del Arte Barlcelona
